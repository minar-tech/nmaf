(75) What is confirmed for the muhtasib: the prohibition of cheating and under-measuring in weights, scales, and cymbals. He should beware of the measurers and weighers and frighten them with the punishment of God Almighty, and forbid them from cheating and under-measuring. Whenever he discovers treachery from one of them, he should admonish him for that and report him, so that others may be deterred by it (Kuwaiti Jurisprudence Encyclopedia, Vol. 12, p. (145).

The Muhtasib is ordered to remove evil, so he has the right to hold accountable anyone who has committed a sin and punish him for it as he deems appropriate (Kuwaiti Jurisprudence Encyclopedia, Vol. 17, p. (266).
