(23) First: Islam. Islam is a condition for the validity of accountability because it provides authority and the honor of arbitration. The infidel was excluded because he is humiliated and does not deserve the honor of arbitration over the Muslims. God Almighty said: “And never will God grant the disbelievers a way over the believers.” And because in commanding and forbidding there is support for the religion, none of its people are those who deny the origin of the religion (“The Kuwaiti Jurisprudential Encyclopedia,” Vol. 17, p. (235).
