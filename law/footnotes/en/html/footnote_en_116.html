(116) On the authority of Aisha, may God be pleased with her, she said: The Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, said: “Whoever introduces into this matter of ours something that is not part of it, it will be rejected” (Narrated by Al-Bukhari, (2697).

The truth is that the imams who are followed in religion are all on the straight path, so any of their schools of thought that is popular in a country and has many scholars in it, the layman must follow it (I’la’ al-Sunan, vol. 20, p. (290).

Innovation is basically the creation of something that did not exist at the time of the Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace. Innovation is of two types: If it is something that falls under what is desirable in the Sharia, then it is a good innovation. If it falls under what is reprehensible in the Sharia, then it is a reprehensible innovation (Umdat al-Qari Sharh Sahih al-Bukhari, vol. 11, p. 126, publisher: Dar Ihya’ al-Turath al-Arabi, Beirut).
