(24) The second condition: Obligation (puberty and reason): Obligation is a request that involves difficulty and hardship, and its condition is the ability to understand the speech, and the suitability of the obligated person to issue the action from him in the manner required by the Sharia, and its support is the mind, which is the tool of understanding, and God Almighty made it the foundation of religion and the world, so He made obligation obligatory in its entirety.

- Obligation is a condition for the obligation of accountability and assuming guardianship. As for merely commanding and forbidding, the boy is not addressed and is not obligated to do that. As for the possibility of the action and its permissibility in his case, it only requires reason. So if he understands the act of worship and knows the reprehensible and the way to change it, then he volunteers it, it is acceptable (“The Kuwaiti Jurisprudential Encyclopedia,” Vol. 17, p. (235).
