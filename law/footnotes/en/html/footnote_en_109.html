(109) “O you who have believed, decreed upon you is fasting as it was decreed upon those before you that you may become righteous.” (Al-Baqarah, verse (183)

“So whoever among you witnesses the month, let him fast it.” (Al-Baqarah, verse (185)

On the authority of Ibn Umar, may God be pleased with them both, who said: The Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, said: “Islam is built on five: the testimony that there is no god but God and that Muhammad is the Messenger of God, establishing prayer, paying zakat, performing Hajj, and fasting Ramadan” (Narrated by Al-Bukhari, 8; and Muslim, (121).

The Muhtasib is ordered to remove evil, so he has the right to hold accountable anyone who has committed a sin and punish him for it as he deems appropriate (Kuwaiti Jurisprudence Encyclopedia, Vol. 17, p. (266).
