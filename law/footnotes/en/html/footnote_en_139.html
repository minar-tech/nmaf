(139) On the authority of Ali, he said: “I said: O Messenger of God, if something happens to us in which there is no clear command or prohibition, what do you command me to do? He said: ‘Consult the jurists and worshippers about it, and do not follow the opinion of a particular person.’” (Narrated by al-Tabarani in al-Awsat, and its men are trustworthy among the people of Sahih.) (Majma’ al-Zawa’id wa Manba’ al-Fawa’id, Hadith No. 834; Publisher: Maktabat al-Qudsi, Cairo).
