(123) Levels of accountability: Some scholars have mentioned levels of change that can be summarized as follows:

The first type: Warning and reminder, and that is for someone who is known to remove the corruption that occurred due to it occurring suddenly and ignorantly, as occurs from someone who is ignorant of the subtleties of corruption in sales, and the paths of usury that are hidden from him, as well as what occurs from not performing the pillars of prayer and the conditions of worship, so they are forbidden by way of gentleness, kindness and attraction (Islamic Jurisprudence and Its Evidences, Vol. 17, p. 265, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr - Syria - Damascus).
