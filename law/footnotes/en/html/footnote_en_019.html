The relationship of the Imam with the nation or the subjects is that of a faithful servant with his master. He must provide happiness and spread security and prosperity for all, and he must be adorned with the qualities of mercy and sincerity, without coercion or harm (“Islamic Jurisprudence and Its Evidence,” Vol. 8, p. 6313, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr - Syria - Damascus).
