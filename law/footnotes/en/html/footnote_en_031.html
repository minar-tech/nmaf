(31) The muhtasib must be gentle in all his affairs, as the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace, said, as narrated by Abd bin Hamid and Al-Dhiya’ on the authority of Anas: “Gentleness is never in anything without beautifying it, and it is never removed from anything without disfiguring it” (“Islamic Jurisprudence and Its Evidences,” Vol. 8, p. 6263, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr - Syria - Damascus).
