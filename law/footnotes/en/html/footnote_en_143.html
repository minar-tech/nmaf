(143) As for advertising in newspapers, there were no incident papers in the era of the jurists as there is in our time, so there is no research in the books of jurisprudence regarding advertising by this means. However, advertising in our time by means of the aforementioned papers is also permissible. However, since not all people read newspapers and know how to read them, and since there are not newspapers in every place, it is also necessary to announce by means of a crier in gatherings of people, as the jurists said... (Durar al-Hukkam Sharh Majallat al-Ahkam, Vol. 2, p. 249, Publisher: Dar al-Jeel).
