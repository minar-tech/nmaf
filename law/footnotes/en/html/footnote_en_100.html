((100) On the authority of Ibn Abbas, who said: The Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, forbade inciting animals against each other (Sunan Abi Dawood). There is no disagreement among the jurists regarding the prohibition of inciting animals against each other and agitating them against each other, because it is foolishness and leads to harm to the animal, and may lead to its destruction without a legitimate purpose (“The Kuwaiti Jurisprudential Encyclopedia”).
