((97) His saying: “And among those who play with children” in Al-Hindiya. It was narrated on the authority of Abu Al-Hasan that if a sheikh wrestled with young people in gatherings, his testimony would not be accepted. End quote (“Radd Al-Mukhtar ala Al-Durr Al-Mukhtar”, Vol. 7, p. 161, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr for Printing and Publishing - Beirut).
