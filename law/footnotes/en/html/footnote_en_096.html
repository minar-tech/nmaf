(96) “Do you commit such immorality as no one before you has committed among the worlds?” (Al-A’raf, (80).

- On the authority of Abu Hurairah, who said: The Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, said: “Cursed is he who has intercourse with his wife in her anus.” (“Al-Sunan Al-Kubra” by Al-Nasa’i: 8966, and Ahmad ibn Hanbal in his “Musnad”: 10209, and “Marqat Al-Mafatih”: 3193, Vol. 6, p. 321, Book of Marriage, Chapter on Intercourse, Hanafi Library).

- (His saying “or he came from behind”) includes the anus of his wife (“Durar al-Hukkam Sharh Ghurar al-Ahkam”, Vol. 5, p. (308).

There is no disagreement among jurists regarding the prohibition of having intercourse with a dead woman, whether she was his wife or a stranger to him during her lifetime. Ibn Hajar al-Haytami considered it to be one of the major sins and indecencies (“The Kuwaiti Jurisprudential Encyclopedia”, Vol. 44, p. 31, Al-Rashidiya Library).
