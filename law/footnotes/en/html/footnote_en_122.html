(122) Ibn Abidin says: “The punishment varies according to the people, so there is no meaning in determining it when the intended purpose is achieved without it” (The Jurisprudential Encyclopedia, Vol. 12, p. 262, Al-Rashidiya Library).

- He may impose punishment for apparent wrongdoings, but he may not exceed the limits (Kuwaiti Jurisprudence Encyclopedia, Vol. 17, p. 234, Al-Rashidiya Library).
