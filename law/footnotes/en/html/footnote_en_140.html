(140) “And cooperate in righteousness and piety, but do not cooperate in sin and aggression.” (Al-Ma’idah: (2)

- Cooperation between people, strengthening ties between them, and attracting their love: Volunteering to do all kinds of good deeds and kindness spreads cooperation between people, and that is why God called for it in His saying: “And cooperate in righteousness and piety” (The Encyclopedia of Jurisprudence, Vol. 12, p. (151).

- Omar (may Allah be pleased with him) gave a sermon to the Companions saying: If you do not help me, who will? Abu Hurairah said: We will help you. (Al-Farouq, p. 223; Dar Al-Salam for Publishing and Distribution).
