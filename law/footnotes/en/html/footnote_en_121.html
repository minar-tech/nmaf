(121) “The evil is every sin that the Shari’ah has forbidden, whether it is committed by an adult or an unauthorized person.” So whoever sees a boy or an insane person drinking alcohol, he must stop him and pour out his alcohol. And whoever sees an insane person committing adultery with an insane woman or having intercourse with an animal, he must prevent that (Islamic Jurisprudence and Its Evidence, Vol. 8, p. 473, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr - Syria - Damascus).

- Whoever sees a boy or a madman drinking alcohol must pour out his alcohol and stop him. Likewise, if he sees a madman committing adultery with a mad woman or an animal, he must stop him from doing so (Kuwaiti Jurisprudence Encyclopedia, Vol. 17, p. 251, Al-Rashidiya Library).
