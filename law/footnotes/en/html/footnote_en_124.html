(124) Levels of accountability: Some scholars have mentioned levels of change that can be summarized as follows:

The second type: preaching and intimidation from God, and this is for someone who is known to have committed an evil act and he knows about it, from the types of sins that are not hidden from the responsible Muslim, so the one who is responsible for it should take care of him with preaching and intimidation from his Lord (Islamic Jurisprudence and Its Evidences, Vol. 17, p. 265, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr - Syria - Damascus).
