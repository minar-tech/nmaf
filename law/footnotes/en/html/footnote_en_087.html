(87) On the authority of Abu Hurairah, on the authority of the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace, who said: “It is not permissible for a woman who believes in God and the Last Day to travel a day’s journey except with a mahram” (Narrated by Muslim).

- (His statement: “On a journey”) is three days and nights, so it is permissible for her to go out for less than that for a need without a mahram. “Bahr.” It was narrated from Abu Hanifa and Abu Yusuf that it is disliked for her to go out alone for a day’s journey, and the fatwa should be based on this because of the corruption of the time (“Sharh al-Lubab”). This is supported by the hadith in the two Sahihs: “It is not permissible for a woman who believes in Allah and the Last Day to travel for a day and a night’s journey except with a mahram.” In a wording by Muslim: “A night’s journey,” and in another wording: “A day” (“Al-Durr al-Mukhtar and Ibn Abidin’s Commentary” “Radd al-Muhtar,” vol. 2, p. 464, publisher: Dar al-Fikr - Beirut).
