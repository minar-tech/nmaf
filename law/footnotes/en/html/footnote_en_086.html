(86) On the authority of Hamza bin Abi Asid Al-Ansari, on the authority of his father, that he heard the Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, say while he was leaving the mosque, and the men mixed with the women in the street, so the Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, said to the women: “Step back, for it is not for you to crowd the street, stick to the edges of the street.” He said: So the woman would stick to the wall, until her garment would stick to the wall from her sticking to it (Narrated by Abu Dawud: (5272).

- (He said) The judge should present women separately and men separately, because people crowd his council. And in the mixing of women with men in a crowded place there is temptation and ugliness that is not hidden (“Al-Mabsut” by Al-Sarakhsi, Vol. 16, p. 80, Publisher: Dar Al-Ma’rifah - Beirut).
