(34) Abu Saeed said: As for this, he has fulfilled what was required of him. I heard the Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, say: “Whoever among you sees an evil, let him change it with his hand. If he is unable to do so, then with his tongue. If he is unable to do so, then with his heart, and that is the weakest of faith.” (“Sahih Muslim,” Hadith No. 49, Vol. 1, p. 69, Publisher: Dar Ihya’ al-Turath al-Arabi - Beirut).

The calculation is carried out in every good deed if it is apparent that it has been abandoned, and in every evil deed if it is apparent that it has been done (“The Jurisprudential Encyclopedia,” Vol. 17, p. (245).
