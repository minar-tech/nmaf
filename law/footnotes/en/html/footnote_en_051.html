(51) She should not raise her voice when reciting or glorifying God, because the basic condition of women is to cover up, and she is not required to do so, as the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace, said: “And clapping is for women” (“The Kuwaiti Jurisprudential Encyclopedia,” Vol. 12, p. 79, Rashidieh Library - Kuwait).

- It was mentioned in the conditions of prayer that a woman’s voice is ‘awrah according to the most correct opinion, and the discussion of it has passed, so refer to it. (“Al-Durr Al-Mukhtar”, Vol. 6, p. 369, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr for Printing and Publishing - Beirut).
