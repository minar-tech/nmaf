(125) Levels of accountability: Some scholars have mentioned levels of change that can be summarized as follows:

The third type: Reprimand, reprimand, harshness in speech, rebuke with the tongue, severity in threats and denunciation. This is for someone for whom preaching is of no use, and for whom gentle warning does not succeed, but rather shows signs of persistence in evil and mockery of preaching. This should be done in a way that is not considered obscene or extravagant in speech, free of lying, and without attributing to the one who has been advised something that is not true, limited to what is necessary, so that its result is not persistence and arrogance (Islamic Jurisprudence and Its Evidences, Vol. 17, p. 265, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr - Syria - Damascus).
