((119) “Indeed, those who devour the property of orphans unjustly are only consuming fire into their bellies, and they will burn in a Blaze.” (An-Nisa, verse (10)

- On the authority of Anas, may God be pleased with him, who said: The Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, said: “Support your brother, whether he is an oppressor or oppressed.” A man said: O Messenger of God, I support him if he is oppressed, but how do I support him if he is an oppressor? He said: “You restrain him or prevent him from oppression, for that is supporting him.” (Narrated by al-Bukhari, 6952; and al-Tirmidhi, (2255)

On the authority of Abdullah bin Omar, may God be pleased with them both, on the authority of the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace, who said: “Injustice will be darkness on the Day of Resurrection” (Narrated by Al-Bukhari, 2447; Muslim, 6742; and Al-Tirmidhi, (2030).

On the authority of Abu Hurairah, on the authority of the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace, who said: “The best home among Muslims is a home in which there is an orphan who is treated well, and the worst home among Muslims is a home in which there is an orphan who is treated badly.” (Narrated by Ibn Majah, (2653)

On the authority of Abu Hurairah, may God be pleased with him, on the authority of the Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace, who said: “Avoid the seven great destructive sins.” They said: O Messenger of God, what are they? He said: “...and consuming the wealth of an orphan.” (Narrated by Al-Bukhari, (2766)
