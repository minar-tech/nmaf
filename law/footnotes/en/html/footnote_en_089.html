((89) Saeed bin Jubair said: Entering the bath without a loincloth is forbidden. According to the hadith of Jabir bin Abdullah: The Prophet, may God bless him and grant him peace, said: “Whoever believes in God and the Last Day should not enter the bath without a loincloth, and whoever believes in God and the Last Day should not let his wife enter the bath.” It was also narrated: “Whoever enters the bath without a loincloth will be cursed by the two angels” (“The Kuwaiti Jurisprudential Encyclopedia,” Vol. 18, p. 156, Rashidiya Library - Kuwait).
