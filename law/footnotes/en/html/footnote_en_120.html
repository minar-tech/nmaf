(120) “The jurists of Islam have agreed on the necessity of applying Sharia law in the land of Islam to Muslims and others” (Islamic Jurisprudence and Its Evidence, Vol. 8, p. 5973, Publisher: Dar Al-Fikr - Syria - Damascus).

- The gist of it is: The one who is granted protection in our country before he becomes a dhimmi is subject to the same ruling as a dhimmi, except for the obligation of retaliation for killing him, and not being held accountable for punishments other than those in which there is a slave’s right, and taking a tenth from him. We mentioned before this chapter that he is committed to the Muslims’ affairs in the future (Radd al-Muhtar ala al-Durr al-Mukhtar, vol. 3, p. 273, Rashidiya Library).
